South Chicago and Rail Service

A look into two proposed rail projects on the south side of Chicago.

The proposed Red Line Extension(RLE) and Metra Electric(ME) improvements pose an issue for regional leaders: which one is the best use of tax dollars for the residents of the south side of Chicago? This map showcases what the RLE would look like and also what the area looks like currently with the ME line.
